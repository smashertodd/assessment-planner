# 📋 Assessment Planner

A free, simple tool that helps students break down their assessments into manageable steps.

**No login. No app to install. No data leaves the browser.**

Students open the link, answer a few questions, and get a personalised study plan with tasks they can tick off as they go.

---

## ✨ What it does

- **Step-by-step setup wizard** — students pick their assessment type (essay, report, lit review, poster, presentation), set their dates, and choose how much guidance they want
- **Automatic plan generation** — breaks the work into stages with practical tasks
- **Progressive task reveal** — shows one task at a time so students don't feel overwhelmed
- **Three guidance modes** — Essentials (minimal), Guided (tips and sources), Coach (critical thinking prompts)
- **Quick-start profiles** — presets for new students, returning learners, postgrads, ND-friendly, and international students

---

## 🌏 International Student Support

A toggle in the settings adds academic English tips and NZ cultural conventions into every stage of the plan — hedging language, NZ spelling, referencing norms, tikanga Māori awareness, and more.

---

## 🎨 Customisation

Students can also:

- Paste in their **learning outcomes** and **rubric** to get tailored tasks
- Choose from **9 colour themes** (including high contrast and dark mode)
- Turn on **accessibility options** — plain English, ND-friendly spacing, dyslexia-friendly mode
- Add **study days and time blocks** to fit their schedule
- **Export to calendar** (.ics file) or **print** their plan

---

## 🔒 Privacy

Everything runs in the browser. Student data is saved only to their own device using local storage. Nothing is sent to any server.
